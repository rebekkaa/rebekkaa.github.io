About me

I am a tenure-track assistant professor in Software Engineering at Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, Sweden. I am also an adjunct faculty member in the Software and Societal Systems Department (S3D) at Carnegie Mellon University.

I lead a research group that is partially supported by the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP) funded by the Knut and Alice Wallenberg Foundation.
